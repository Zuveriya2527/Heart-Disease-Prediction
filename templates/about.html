What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease refers to a group of medical conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels. One of the most common types is coronary artery disease, which occurs when arteries become narrowed due to cholesterol buildup.

Common symptoms include chest pain, fatigue, shortness of breath, and discomfort in the upper body. Early diagnosis and healthy lifestyle choices can significantly reduce risk.
